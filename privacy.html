Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how OnlyMedia (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use N8n-Drive-FK-Lion to manage and publish videos to Facebook Pages you administer.

What we collect

  • Facebook Page information (Page IDs, names) strictly for posting on your behalf.
  • Access tokens you provide to enable posting; stored securely in our automation platform (n8n) or your infrastructure.
  • Operational metadata (timestamps, file names) needed to process and log posts.
  • We do not collect end-user personal data.

How we use information

To authenticate with Facebook, upload videos to your Page(s), prevent duplicates, and provide basic logs and troubleshooting.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers strictly to operate the service (e.g., hosting).

Data retention

Operational logs and tokens are retained only as long as necessary to run the service or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your choices

  • Revoke Facebook permissions at any time via Facebook settings.
  • Request access or deletion by emailing usmanyear1@gmail.com.
